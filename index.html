Erroll Wood

I am a Principal Scientist at Microsoft's Mixed-Reality and AI Lab in Cambridge, UK. There, I have worked on hand tracking for HoloLens 2, avatars for Microsoft Mesh, synthetic data for face tracking, and Holoportation. Before, I did my PhD at the University of Cambridge, supervised by Professor Peter Robinson.
